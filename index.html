About Me

My name is Jose Barajas, but everyone calls me Tony. I was born in Mexico but was transplanted into the United States at an early age. I have lived in Utah for the better part of 24 years so I definitely consider myself a Utahn and all the perks that come with it, fry-sauce comes to mind. Something most people would not know about me is that I am also Basque, Basque is a culture located right in between Spain and France, and I embrace this culture wholeheartedly. The Basque culture is rich with delicious food, dancing , and ancient traditions.

Sci-fi movies and cheesy 80's action movies are my favorite like "Commando". I enjoy the outdoors and consider myself lucky to live in such a beautiful state. I know that when someone asks "what type of music do you like?" if someone answers that they like "everything" that they're just not beign honest. Yet I feel compelled to say that I do like everything from classical to metal music, from dub step to country music. I find that exposing yourself to new things can lead to great discoveries.
